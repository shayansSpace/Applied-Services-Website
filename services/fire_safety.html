Fire Safety Services

We provide comprehensive fire safety solutions to protect lives, property, and operations. Our services include the supply, installation, and maintenance of fire extinguishers, smoke and heat detectors, fire alarms, hose reels, hydrants, and gas-based suppression systems. We also install emergency lighting, exit signage, and fire safety control panels to ensure safe evacuation during emergencies. Our team conducts fire risk assessments, system inspections, and provides fire drills and training to ensure regulatory compliance and preparedness. Whether it's a petrol pump, office, or industrial site, we deliver reliable, code-compliant fire safety systems tailored to your environment and safety requirements.
