Tank Cleaning Services

We offer specialized cleaning services for petrol and kerosene oil storage tanks, ensuring safety, operational efficiency, and regulatory compliance. Our process includes safe drainage, degassing, and vapor removal to eliminate flammable fumes. Trained professionals remove sludge, sediment, and hydrocarbon buildup using high-pressure washing and chemical cleaning techniques. We follow strict safety protocols, using gas detectors and PPE to operate in hazardous environments. After thorough cleaning and drying, we ensure the tank is residue-free and ready for safe use. Our services cover underground and above-ground tanks, supporting fuel stations, depots, and industrial clients with reliable and efficient tank maintenance.

Disinfection (for water tanks)
Sludge & Waste Disposal
Drying & Final Rinse
